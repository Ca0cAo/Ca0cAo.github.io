Introduction

Subtitle Edit is a free (open source) editor for video subtitles - a subtitle editor :) With SE you can easily adjust a subtitle if it is out of sync with the video in several different ways. You can also use SE for making new subtitles from scratch (do use the time-line/waveform/spectrogram) or translating subtitles. Moreover, the app supports over 250+ types of subtitle formats and is available in 34 languages.

This site will show how to create a basic subtitle for a given video from scratch.
